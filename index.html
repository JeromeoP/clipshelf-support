Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open ClipShelf?

Press ⌘⇧V (Command+Shift+V) from anywhere, or click the menu bar icon.

Where is my clipboard data stored?

Everything is stored locally in ~/Library/Application Support/Jerome.Clipboard/

Does ClipShelf sync to the cloud?

No. All data stays on your Mac. This is by design for privacy.

How do I delete all history?

Open the three-dot menu → Delete → Clear All History

Can I change the keyboard shortcut?

Yes, go to Settings and customize the global hotkey.
